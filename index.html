About the Network

THAWNETWORK aggregates community-reported detention center activity, ICE operations, checkpoints, and protest actions across the United States.

All data is publicly sourced, crowd-verified, and displayed in near real-time so communities can stay informed and respond quickly to developments near them.

01
Community Reports
Members submit observations via the forum with location, type, status, and description.
02
Verification
Moderators cross-check submissions and mark entries verified before they go live.
03
Live Map & Data
Verified entries populate the map and database automatically, refreshing every 90 seconds.
